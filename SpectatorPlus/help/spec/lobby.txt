Sets or deletes the spectators lobby.

The spectators lobby is a location, defined with this
command, where the spectators are teleported when they
enter or leave the spectator mode, or when they leave
an arena.

§6/spec lobby set§r: sets the lobby at your current
position. You have to be a player to do that.
§6/spec lobby delete§r: deletes the lobby. The players
will no longer be teleported anywhere when the spectator
mode is enabled or disabled.
